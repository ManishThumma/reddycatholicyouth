Faith, Family & Telugu Roots

🪔 Where We Come From

Our family is from Telangana and Andhra Pradesh. Telugu Catholics whose grandparents prayed the same rosary every evening, whose uncles got married in the same village church, who grew up hearing the same feast day songs year after year. That did not stay behind when we boarded the flight. It is in us. It travels with us.

Telangana & Andhra Pradesh

✝ Catholic by Generations

Our Catholic faith is not something recent. For generations this family has lived it. Sunday Mass without question. The Rosary before bed. The feast of Our Lady treated like a real holiday. Getting on a plane to America did not change any of that. If anything it made it mean more, because now it is one of the few things that still feels like home.

🇺🇸 Why This Exists

When you first land in America, whether for a Masters program or a new job, you can feel alone very quickly. The food is different, the people are different, and everyone you know is twelve time zones away. This map exists so you know that somewhere in Houston or Atlanta or Chicago there is a cousin who speaks Telugu, who went through the exact same thing, and who will pick up the call.

🔒 Private and Exclusive

This is not a public directory. Every person on this map was personally verified by the admin. You have to be an actual cousin from our extended family to be here. Your information stays within the family and is never shared with anyone outside it.
